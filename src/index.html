Prince

Prince was a world famous rockstar and song writer. In 2016, he fell into a coma, before dying in his apartment later that year. Upon further investigation, the cause of his death was attributed to an overdose of fentanyl in his liver. This chemical likely came from the massive amounts of prescription medication he was taking.
